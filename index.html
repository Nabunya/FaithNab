Comments

In general,here we investigate whether districts that voted for a particular party in the presidential election voted in a similar manner at the Parliamentary.

The irregularities are seen where a political party performed well in the presidential votes but underperformed in the parliamentary. This can be because;

  • When a particular party is voted for presidential more than parliamentary and vice versa. Best example;-Nup where in some districts, Voters voted for Robert Kyagulanyi at presidential level more than they voted nup mps .
  • There were some missing results from a few districts.
  • Political party did not run in every district at the parliamentary level.
  • And when perhaps a particular political party is popular in some districts and not others.
